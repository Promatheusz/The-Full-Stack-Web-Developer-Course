My Top 3 movies

Iron man

A billionaire industrialist and genius inventor, Tony Stark (Robert Downey Jr.), is kidnapped and forced to build a devastating weapon. Instead, using his intelligence and ingenuity, Tony builds a high-tech suit of armor and escapes captivity.


Avengers: End game

After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War, the universe is in ruins. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe.


Black Panther

T'Challa, heir to the hidden but advanced kingdom of Wakanda, must step forward to lead his people into a new future and must confront a challenger from his country's past.


Spider-man

Peter Parker, a high school student, gains spider-like abilities, which he must use to fight crime as Spider-Man while dealing with the everyday struggles of adolescence.
